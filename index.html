About Me

A lifelong experimenter and tinkerer. Since I was young I have enjoyed building computers, designing radio antennas, and experimenting with technology. I also enjoy camping, traveling and hunting.


I grew up in the great state of Wisconsin, and I currently live near Kansas City. I graduated from a Olivet Nazarene University in Illinois in 2012 with a B.S. in Criminal Justice. After that I spent 4 years in the Army as a Logistics Officer. The majority of my service was at Fort Riley, KS and southern Afghanistan. After leaving the army I was a distribution center manager, but I decided to pursue my lifelong dream of working with tech and computers!
